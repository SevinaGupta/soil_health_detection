MOISTURE SENSOR

Soil moisture is basically the content of water present in soil. It can measure the moisture content in the soil based on the change in resistance between the two conducting plates.The Soil Moisture Sensor uses capacitance to measure the water content of soil (by measuring the dielectric permittivity of the soil, which is a function of the water content). Simply insert this rugged sensor into the soil to be tested,and the volumetric water content of the soil is reported in percent.
